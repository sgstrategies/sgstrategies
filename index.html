Our Process

The PFS System

Every revenue-generating campaign we run follows the same battle-tested framework. The PFS system ensures your SMS and ad strategy is built on solid ground, executed with precision, and scaled intelligently.

P
P

Position — Define the Offer & Audience

Before we write a single message or set a single ad objective, we get crystal clear on who we're talking to and what we're saying. We audit your current customer data, identify your highest-value segments, map out the customer journey, and define the exact offer that will make people act. No guessing — just precision targeting from day one.

Audience Research Customer Segmentation Offer Validation Competitor Ad Analysis Channel Selection
F
F

Funnel — Build the Revenue Machine

With the audience and offer locked in, we engineer the full funnel — from the first touchpoint to the purchase. For SMS, this means flows, sequences, and segmentation logic. For ads, this means campaign structure, creative angles, landing pages, and retargeting layers. Everything is built to work together seamlessly.

SMS Flow Architecture Ad Campaign Structure Landing Page Strategy Retargeting Setup Creative Concepts
S
S

Scale — Optimize & Amplify

Once campaigns are live, we don't sit back — we iterate fast. A/B testing ad creatives and SMS copy, analyzing open rates and ROAS daily, killing what doesn't work and doubling down on what does. When a winning combination is found, we increase budget and scale strategically to maximize revenue without breaking what's working.
